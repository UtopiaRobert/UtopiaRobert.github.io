Overview

This page highlights a few of the hands-on projects I’ve been involved with—each rooted in service, ecological awareness, and personal transformation. These projects are expressions of healing in action.

Intentional Community

A land-based healing space guided by shared values of spiritual growth, mutual support, and sustainable living. We welcome others to co-create this sanctuary in service to the Earth and one another.

Explore Community Vision

Tiny Homes & Alternative Shelters

Designing and living in off-grid tiny homes built from natural and repurposed materials. These spaces are sacred, simple, and deeply integrated into the desert landscape.

Permaculture & Desert Restoration

Land regeneration projects focused on rainwater harvesting, soil building, food forests, and climate resilience in arid ecosystems. These efforts are ongoing experiments in listening to the land.
