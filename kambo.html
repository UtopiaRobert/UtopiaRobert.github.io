The Story of Kambo

Kambo medicine — also known as Sapo — is a powerful secretion from the Phyllomedusa bicolor frog of the Amazon. Indigenous tribes call it the “vaccine of the forest,” and have used it for centuries to purify the body, awaken intuition, and increase vitality.

The practice spread globally after investigative journalist Peter Gorman introduced it to the outside world in the 1980s.
